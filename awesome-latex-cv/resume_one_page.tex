\par{
  {\textbf{About.}}
  I hold a PhD in Aerospace Engineering from the Indian Institute of
  Technology Bombay, where I focused on mesh-free methods for solving
  partial differential equations. I have postdoctoral research
  experience at the University of Surrey, Indian Institute of Technology
  Delhi, and currently at DWI Leibniz Institute for Interactive
  Materials, working on particle dispersion, battery degradation, and
  bioprinting processes. My expertise includes SPH, DEM, FEM, and FVM,
  with a strong focus on developing scalable, high-performance
  computational methods for large-scale and exascale systems.
