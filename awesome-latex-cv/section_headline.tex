{\textbf{Bio.}}

I am an MTech+PhD candidate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay,
India, working under the supervision of Prof Prabhu Ramachandran. My work
broadly is a study of partial differential equations through mesh-free methods.

{\textbf{Research interests.}} My Ph.D. research work covers a range of topics:
\textbf{Meshless methods}: Smoothed particle hydrodynamics, Discrete element
method, High-performance computing, Rigid-fluid coupling, Fluid-structure
interaction, Elastic-plastic modeling.
