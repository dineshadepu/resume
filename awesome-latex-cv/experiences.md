At RWTH Aachen University, I am currently working on ultrasound-assisted 3D bioprinting, focusing on the simulation of acoustically driven fluid–particle systems for controlled cell patterning. My work involves developing GPU-accelerated solvers for acoustic fluid dynamics using the Finite Volume Method, coupled with particle-based models to capture cell motion and interactions within structured ultrasound fields.
